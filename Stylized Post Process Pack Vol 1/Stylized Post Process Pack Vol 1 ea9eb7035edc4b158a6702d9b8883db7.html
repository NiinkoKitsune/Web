How to implement Post Process :

Firstly, add a Post Process Volume :

A 3D wireframed cube appears on the scene, it's the Post Process Volume.

  1. Click on the Wireframed cube in the scene or search for it in the Outliner.
  1. In the details, click on the PostProcessVolume.
  1. In the search bar, type "Material".
  1. In the Post Process Materials section, click on the "+" button in the Array.
  1. A "Choose" drop-down panel will appear, click on it and select Asset reference.
  1. Click on the drop-down panel labeled "None" and search for "PP_Toon_Inst" or "PP_HandDraw_Inst".

Now, enjoy your beautiful world.
